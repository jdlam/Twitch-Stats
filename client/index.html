TwitchFlare

A Visual Representation of Twitch.tv's Top Games

Data provided by Twitch.tv

  • Inner Ring: Games on Twitch.tv
  • Outer Ring: Streamers

About

This app is meant to help visualize and simplify comparisons between games, allowing one to see trends throughout the past 24 hours. Each section of the graph is either a top game, or a top streamer, on Twitch.tv at that point in time.

In the past, I've done analyses on the potential of advertising on the Twitch platform, but struggled finding relevant and data. When I did find relevant data, it was very difficult to decipher. Social Blade lists them all in a chart, without giving options on choosing for specific games. TwitchApps has a similar issue, where it's difficult to find data on a specific game.

The above data is pulled directly from Twitch.tv at different hours of the day. Currently, it is set to pull once an hour, and limited to the top 6 games. From each game, it then pulls in data about the top 10 streams online. All of this data is stored on a personal database, which is then pushed into the Sunburst Chart.
